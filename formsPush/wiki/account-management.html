Your Account

APEX Sport uses a simple account system for personalized features.

Creating an Account

Navigate to the login page via the main store and select "Register". Fill in your name, email, and password to create an account.

Logging In

Enter your email and password on the Login page. Once logged in, you will be redirected to the homepage.

Password Requirements

Choose a password you will remember. When registering, both password fields must match before your account is created.

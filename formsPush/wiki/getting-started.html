Welcome to APEX Sport

APEX Sport is an online store for premium fitness and sports equipment. This guide will walk you through the basics of navigating the site.

Homepage

The homepage features our latest collection and a direct link to the shop. Use the navigation bar at the top to access any section of the site.

Navigation

The top navigation bar includes links to Shop, About, and Help. On mobile devices, tap the hamburger menu icon to open the mobile navigation.

Search

Click the search icon in the navigation bar to open the search overlay. Type a product name, category, or keyword to find what you need.

Theme Switching

APEX Sport supports three visual themes: Default (light), Dark, and Midnight. You can switch themes from the About page or any page with the theme switcher. Your preference is saved automatically.

Finding Products

Visit the Shop page to see all 20 products in our catalogue.

Filter by Category

Use the filter buttons at the top of the shop page to narrow products by category: Strength, Cardio, Recovery, or Accessories. Click "All" to see everything.

Product Cards

Each product card shows the product name, category, selected option, and price. Click any card to view the full product detail page.

Product Detail Page

The detail page shows a larger product view, full description, option details, and an "Add to Cart" button. Use the breadcrumb links at the top to navigate back.

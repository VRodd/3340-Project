Shipping Info

Shipping Policy

We ship across Canada from our Windsor, Ontario warehouse. Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express shipping (2–3 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Free Shipping

Orders over $150 qualify for free standard shipping anywhere in Canada. No promo code needed — the discount is applied automatically at checkout.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Use the tracking number on Canada Post or the courier website to monitor your delivery.

International Shipping

We do not currently ship outside of Canada. We are exploring US shipping options for a future update.

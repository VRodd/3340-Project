Our Story

APEX Sport was founded with a single goal: to provide serious athletes with equipment that matches their intensity. We believe that the gear you train with should never be the limiting factor in your performance.

Based in Windsor, Ontario, our team sources and tests every product in our catalogue. From adjustable dumbbells to percussion massage guns, each item is selected for durability, function, and value.

What We Offer

Our catalogue features 20 premium products across four categories: Strength, Cardio, Recovery, and Accessories. Every product comes in a carefully selected premium option — we cut the entry-level filler so you only see gear worth buying.

Our Mission

Train harder. Go further. We exist to remove the friction between you and your best performance. No fluff, no gimmicks — just equipment that works as hard as you do.

COMP 3340 Project

This website was developed as a group project for COMP 3340 (World Wide Web Information Systems Design) at the University of Windsor. It demonstrates a fully functional e-commerce front-end built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, featuring responsive design, dynamic product pages, cart functionality, theme switching, and a comprehensive help wiki.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you create an account or place an order, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. We also collect browsing data through cookies to improve your experience.

Your information is used to process orders, send order confirmations, and improve the site. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies to store your cart, theme preference, and login session. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not function correctly.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information during transmission and storage.

Using the Cart

Click the bag icon in the navigation to go to your cart at any time.

Adding Items

From any product detail page, click "Add to Cart" to add the item. A toast notification will confirm the action. Items persist in your cart between visits using browser storage.

Editing Quantities

On the cart page, use the + and − buttons next to each item to change the quantity. The subtotal updates automatically.

Removing Items

Click the × button on the right side of any cart item to remove it.

Order Summary

The cart page displays your subtotal, estimated tax (13% HST), and total. The checkout feature will be available in a future update.
